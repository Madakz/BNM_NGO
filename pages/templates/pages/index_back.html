About Breeding New Minds

An Initiative working on renewing the mindset of youths and bridging the gap as regards to Health, Entrepreneurship and Skill Acquisition. Our Organization act as intermediaries reaching out on their behalf.

Vision

To create a platform that bridges the gap between individuals in need and the help givers. To serve as intermediaries actively reaching out on behalf of those seeking help. By doing so the initiative aims to efficiently and effectively connect those in need with appropriate support and resources. Fostering a sense of community and support for vulnerable individuals.

Mission

To provide coordinated, efficient and timely humanitarian relief for those in need. This covers physical, psycological, mental and emotional aspects. With the primary aim of saving lives and alleviating the suffering of vulnerable individuals.
